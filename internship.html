Content Writer

The ideal applicant is passionate about writing and creative, with the ability to develop effective marketing strategies and contribute to business expansion. You will be in charge of coming up with intriguing and appealing stories for both print and digital media.

About
  • Duration: 2 months internship & Stipend is paid for all interns.

Job Responsibilities:

  • Conduct research on issues relating to your sector to come up with ideas and compile data for content creation.
  • Ensure that the material complies with the brand's voice, style, and messaging by working together with the marketing team.
  • To ensure text is clear, consistent, and grammatically correct, proofread and edit it.
  • Include relevant keywords and phrases when optimising material for search engines.
  • To contribute to content strategy and generate original ideas, use creative thinking.
  • Keep abreast of market developments and content writing best practises.

Requirements:

  1. Outstanding writing and editing abilities, as well as a good sense of grammar.
  2. Have the capacity to change one's writing style to appeal to various media and audiences.
  3. The basic knowledge of keyword optimisation and SEO techniques. To create compelling content, you need creativity and the capacity to think beyond the box.
  4. Strong ability to manage time and meet deadlines.
  5. Desire to learn from criticism and to develop as a content writer.
  6. Knowledge with digital publishing platforms and content management systems is a plus.
